Focus problems

It's possible you just need to get yourself organized. Buy a better alarm clock. Experiment with planner apps to find one that works for you. Remember all that organizational advice that the B students needed in high school, but you could blow off because high school was easy? College is harder, so now that advice applies to you as well.

But, also, this could be one of the other problems in disguise.

  • Could you be in the wrong major?
  • Are you overloaded?
  • Could you have a minor disability issue slowing you down?

All of these can mess up your ability to stay organized and keep your focus.
